Why My Swims?

My Swims allows you to record your daily swims while the workout is still fresh on your mind! Right from your mobile device, you can easily record the entire workout or just the yardage.

Track your Goals!

My Swim allows you to track your swim goals. Here's just a few examples of goals:

  • How many yards for the year
  • Best time for 100 free
  • Most yards in a single workout
